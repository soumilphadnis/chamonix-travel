Top Three Activities to Do at Chamonix-Mont-Blanc

Aiguille du Midi

Accessible by a two-stage cable car ride from Chamonix town centre, the trip to the top is an adventure itself. Reaching an altitude of 3,842 metres.

Mer de Glace

Here, you will descend into the heart of the glacier itself. Discover an under-glacier museum, filled with intricate ice sculptures. A truly unique experience!

Train du Montenvers

Starting from Chamonix, this historic cogwheel train takes you up through beautiful pine forests to Montenvers, situated at 1,913 metres.
